This is a Project that is written by C-Sharp and it shows mechanism of a pyramidcompany. 
Description of Proj: 
Operation of this company start with one person. every person add two people in every month. In 6th month of a person, adding people will be stopped and he\she won't add any person.
*How many months do people of iran(population of iran: 70 milion) add to company ??
*How much people of company will rich be ?

Tip : The people will be rich that have at least 6 people in subcategory
and be in company at least 4 months.
